Summary about Grow
Growth stocks" typically refer to shares of companies expected to grow at an above-average rate compared to other companies. These firms usually reinvest their earnings to fuel expansion, rather than paying dividends. Investors are attracted to growth stocks for their potential to deliver substantial capital gains over time. Characteristics of such companies often include innovative products or services, strong market positioning, and robust revenue and earnings growth. However, investing in growth stocks can also carry higher risk due to their typically higher valuation multiples and potential volatility. It's important for investors to conduct thorough research and consider their risk tolerance before investing in growth stocks.
